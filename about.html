why me?

You ask? I deliver! I am a very proactive and resourceful person, that is very passionate about the work I do. But if I could only choose one word to describe myself it would be "creative". Creativity and I are a package deal, and I will sure use it as a tool to create accessible, functional and innovative design to improve the user experience according to their needs.
