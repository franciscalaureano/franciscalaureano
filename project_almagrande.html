O Alma Grande

web design

front-end

academic project

For this project, we had to create an analog and digital object to tell one of the stories given by the professors. My group mates and I choose “O Alma Grande”, by Miguel Torga, for this project.

For the analog project we created a small book. We decided to accompany the text with some charcoal drawings, to represent the era of the story and the people represented in it. These people were simple and poor, so we tried to transmite it in the drawing.

For the digital project, we created a website that interact with the scroll. Scrolling down the website, we can see some parts of the story accompanied by background sounds characterizing the environment and some drawings similar to those in the book.

(website: https://student.dei.uc.pt/~laureano/P3_alma_grande_final )

This project was carried out in the third year of the Design and Multimedia course at FCTUC, for the curricular unit “Projeto 3 - Aplicações Multimédia”, supervised by the professors Pedro Martins, Ana Boavida and Luís Pereira, and was created in collaboration with Carla Gomes and Francisca Sousa.
