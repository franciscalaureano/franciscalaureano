Balada Tipográfica

web design

front-end

academic project

"Balada Tipográfica" was designed with the intention of creating a non-conventional map. The purpose of this map is for people to be drawn to a particular place by the sensations and emotions felt by each person who has passed by.

There are two ways to interact with the map: There is the option to insert a word describing the place where the person is, and the option to explore the map through the words left by other people. To better understand the project, you can watch the demo video through this link. website: https://student.dei.uc.pt/~joanasousa/p4/

This academic work was done in the third year of studies in Design and Multimedia at the FCTUC, for the curriculum unit "Projeto 4 - Multimédia Interativo". This academic work was supervised by professors Pedro Martins, Luís Pereira and Paula da Silva, in collaboration with Joana Sousa and Francisca Sousa.
