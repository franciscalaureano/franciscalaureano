"Data Set Go" is the first Data Science & Machine Learning Hackathon, in Coimbra. This event was created by jeKnowledge and JEST, two junior companies based in Coimbra.

In 2020, they invited me to be part of the 2021 team, by jeKnowledge, to design the Hackathon's identity, which I created in collaboration with Manuel Masseno, a designer from JEST.
